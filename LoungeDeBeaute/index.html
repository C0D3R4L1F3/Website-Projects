We specialize in creating the most hyper-realistic looking brows. Carefully place hair like strokes to create the most natural looking brows. We care about our clients and our goal is to ensure 100% satisfaction and feeling beautiful before they walk out the door.

MEET THE ARTIST

Duyen (Kim) Dinh studied at one of the worlds most prestigious microblading academy in the world (Phibrow Academy). She is PhiBrow certified; specializing in the manual technique of Microblading / 3D Eyebrow / Microstroking / Feathering. She also has her Bloodborne Pathogen Certificate. Her training and attention to detail allows her to create the most realistic hair like brow tattoo with precision. What sets her apart from conventional artist is her passion to continuously learn and adapt to changing trends and styles.

Every client is unique, so should your brows. Kim adapts new style and technique to each set of brows. She will work with you from the beginning to the end and ensure you are 100% satisfied.

People suffering from alopecia, or had side effects of hair loss through chemo will benefit greatly from microblading, as well as people who simply aren’t happy with their eyebrows.
